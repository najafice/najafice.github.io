    Modelling Initial Elastic Modulus of Sand-EPS Mixtures Using Evolutionary Polynomial Regression
    39th National Congress and 4th International Congress of Earth Sciences
    Investigated the use of expanded polystyrene (EPS) in soil stabilization, developing an EPR-based model to predict the initial elastic modulus of sand-EPS composites through triaxial compression tests, and conducted sensitivity and parametric analyses to understand the impact of confining pressure and EPS content on compressibility behavior.

    3rd International Conference on Civil Engineering, Architecture, and Urban Management in Iran
